--- title: Topical fun? Maybe! ---

I got sent an email which asked what you had got up to over the last year. This site takes that idea and serves it up in a nice, fun little package. Each of your "crimes" will cost you a fine. Go through the list admitting to all the bad things you've done and at the end, see how much you owe.

Aptly named Dirty Bungle!

A work in progress but fun all the same.
